Our Story

Osteria 60 is an extravagant version of a Milanese Trattoria. The textured gold walls and bronze pendants create a decadent atmosphere in which you can enjoy the vibrant and colourful cuisine of our critically acclaimed chef Ivan Simeoli. Mixing Italian traditions with innovative techniques, Osteria 60 is a new addition to modern Italian cuisine.
